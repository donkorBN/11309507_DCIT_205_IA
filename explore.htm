Research Areas

We conduct industry standard research in areas such as Biostatistics, AI and Machine Learning and it's application in Health, Climate and Agriculture. We ensure our research sees to benefit our country, it's people and it's economy, and the world at large.

Awards

Our department has a rich history of receiving numerous awards and accolades for our outstanding research, teaching, and service. Some of our most notable achievements include:

  • Oracle Award: 2021 ORACLE Award For Best Industry Standard Curriculum
  • Huawei Eco Award: Best Innovations and Solutions In Tech Climate
  • GES Award: 1st University in the National Informatics Competition

We are proud of these accomplishments and they reflect our commitment to excellence in education and research. Our dedicated faculty and staff have worked tirelessly to earn these awards and recognition. Congratulations to all our award winners!
